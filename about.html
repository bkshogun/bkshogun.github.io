Founded in 2012 by Michael de Gaetano, the name Shogun Travel came after he was moved by one particular character in The Last Dragon and thought that Sho'nuff, the Shogun of Harlem, was pretty badass.

It has nothing to do with the agency, Mr. de Gaetano just loves to travel, jump out of stuff, jump into stuff, and climb other stuff... and maybe fancies himself a shogun.
